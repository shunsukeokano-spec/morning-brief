# Shun's Interest Profile

Edit this file directly when your interests shift. It is read every morning and shapes the "Your Focus" stories in the brief.

## Who I am
Japan-based. Interested in understanding how the world works — geopolitics, technology shifts, economic forces, and where they intersect. Goal: build a picture of where things are heading 6–12 months from now.

## Core interests

- **International politics**: US–China relations, Japan's foreign policy, trade tensions, alliance dynamics (Quad, NATO, ASEAN), elections in major economies
- **Technology**: AI capabilities and what becomes newly possible each month, semiconductor supply chains, hardware innovation, Big Tech strategy
- **Economy**: Central bank policy, currency moves (especially JPY), inflation, energy markets, trade flows affecting Asia
- **Startups & venture**: Funding trends, which sectors are attracting capital, Japan's startup ecosystem
- **AI specifically**: What frontier models can do now vs. last month, agentic AI, AI policy and regulation globally

## What I want flagged

- Anything that could materially affect Japan's position (economic, political, security)
- Signals that a trend is accelerating or reversing — not just what happened, but what it means in 3–6 months
- Underreported stories from Asia, Global South, or non-English media that Western outlets are missing

## What I don't need

- Celebrity tech news or product launches without strategic significance
- Incremental earnings reports unless they signal a larger trend
